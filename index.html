Introduction

Hello! I'm Nana Kwabena Inkum, but most people call me Nana. I'm currently a junior at Lewis University majoring in Computer Science. I was born and raised in Ghana, West Africa, and moved to the United States to pursue my education.

Back home in Ghana, I'm the first in my family to study abroad, which makes this journey even more special. I chose Computer Science because technology has always fascinated me, and I believe it's the key to solving many of the world's problems.

What I Hope to Learn in this Course

  • Best practices for writing clean code
  • Working effectively in development teams using Agile
  • Understanding the full software development lifecycle
  • How to deploy applications to Customers

Fun Fact

A uniquely me fact is that in Ghanaian culture, names have special meanings. My middle name "Kwabena" means "born on Tuesday" in the Akan language, which is actually true I was born on a Tuesday! This tradition connects me to my heritage every time I introduce myself.
